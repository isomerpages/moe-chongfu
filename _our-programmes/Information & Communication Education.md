<h4>OUR VISION &amp; GOALS</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Cultivate a community of learners who actively use technology to enhance
their learning</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide opportunities for students to exercise cyber wellness to use technology
in a safe and discerning manner.</p>
</li>

technology.</p>

<h4>OUR PROGRAMMES</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Integration of SLS</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The SLS is an online learning portal providing equal access to quality
curriculum-aligned resources in major subjects from primary to pre-university
level, in line with the development of 21st Century Competencies (e21CC).
With tools that help to make students’ thinking processes visible, there
can be better targeted intervention to address any gaps in understanding.</p>
<p>The SLS is continually being developed in response to the needs of students
and teachers. Curriculum-aligned resources and system tools are continually
being improved and developed in line with suggestions and feedback from
teachers and students to cater to students’ diverse and evolving learning
needs.</p>

<p>Our level programmes from Primary 1 to Primary 6 are designed to equip
every student with the nine digital competencies under the “Find, Think,
Apply, Create” framework of Edtech Masterplan 2030, preparing our students
to be future-ready individuals. By incorporating computational thinking
and integrating new media literacies across various subjects, the programme
aims to help students:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Develop computational thinking and problem-solving abilities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Acquire and apply digital skills to create digital content</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Exhibit 21st-century competencies through self-directed and collaborative
learning</p>
</li>
</ul>
